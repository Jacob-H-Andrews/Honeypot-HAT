How to buy Bitcoin (BTC)

For the safety and security of users, this guide will inform on how to buy Bitcoin without ID (identification).


As legislators in the US and around the globe bit by bit present more tightly guidelines on cryptographic money exchanging, it's getting increasingly hard to purchase crypto without ID.

However, if you don’t have suitable proof of ID or if you simply value your privacy, there are still some sites where you can buy cryptocurrency without completing a verification process.

Why use Bitcoin?
Bitcoin empowers designers to manufacture decentralized applications. A decentralized application or Dapp fill some specific need to its clients. Bitcoin, for instance, is a Dapp that furnishes its clients with a distributed electronic money framework that empowers online Bitcoin installments. Since decentralized applications are comprised of code that sudden spikes in demand for a blockchain arrange, they are not constrained by any individual or focal substance.

Because decentralized applications run on the blockchain, they benefit from all of its properties.

Immutability – A third party cannot make any changes to data.
Corruption & tamper proof – Apps are based on a network formed around the principle of consensus, making censorship impossible.
Secure – With no central point of failure and secured using cryptography, applications are well protected against hacking attacks and fraudulent activities.
Zero downtime – Apps never go down and can never be switched off.


Buying Bitcoin without ID verification
Some exchanges have different thresholds for verification requirements:

  • LocalBitcoins lets you purchase Bitcoin without confirming ID.
  • Paxful doesn't require ID confirmation for account creation, yet requires check for accounts that arrive at what might be compared to US$1,500 in exchange volume or wallet movement.
