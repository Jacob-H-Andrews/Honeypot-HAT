All About Cryptocurrency

What is cryptocurrency?
A cryptocurrency is a digital commodity built to serve as an exchanging mechanism that uses powerful cryptography to encrypt financial transfers, monitoring of additional unit development and to verify commodity transfer. In comparison to the centralised digital money and central banking structures, cryptocurrencies utilise decentralised regulation.

How do cryptocurrency transactions work?
Transfers are transmitted between peers using "cryptocurrency wallets" apps. The individual making the transaction uses the wallet apps to pass deposits from one account to another.
